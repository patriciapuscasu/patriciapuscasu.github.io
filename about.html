Who are we?

Museum 540 was founded by Ana Popescu and Cristi Horea, both graduates of BA and MA programmes at the University of Art and Design in Cluj-Napoca. Underwhelmed by the already-existing art spaces in the city, they set out to establish a place that could awaken the desire for art and creation in people’s hearts, as well as propel Romania on the European cultural map.

What makes this museum special lays precisely in its name, as it opens at 5:40 in the morning, the earliest opening time of any museum in the world. Ana and Cristi proposed this idea believing that the perfect start to one’s day could be represented by a visit to the museum at the crack of dawn, right before work or school, or even right after a night of partying.

The first and only exhibitions so far, included the works of Dana Maria and Sara Pop, two of the most resounding names in the city’s art scape, as well as RF Studio, a South African artist and one of Ana and Cristi’s personal favourites.

Museum 540 or simply 540, had its opening on the 10th of January 2020, but closed shortly after following the pandemic. Behind closed doors, no one took a rest, but rather planned and prepared a spectacular re-opening for 2021.
